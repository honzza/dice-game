RULES and SCORING

Your score must be 350 or higher. If your last roll didn't score any points you lose all your previously gained points. If all dice are selected you have to continue rolling.

5 = 50, 1 = 100, doubles (e.g. 22-44-55) = 1000, 123456 = 1000
